Privacy Policy

At ImageFuse, we are committed to safeguarding your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains our stance on information collection, use, and sharing when you use our services.

Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information such as your name, email address, or other details. Your use of our services is completely anonymous.

How We Use Your Information

Since we do not collect personal information, there is no personal data to use. However, we do utilize AI to scan uploaded images and files for illegal activities and violations of our platform rules.

Sharing Your Information

As we do not collect any personal information, there is no data to share. We do not sell, rent, or disclose any personal data to third parties.

Your Rights

Since no personal information is collected, there is no personal data to access, correct, or delete. Please note that once images are uploaded, they cannot be deleted from our system.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
